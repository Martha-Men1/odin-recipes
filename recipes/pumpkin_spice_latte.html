Pumpkin Spice Latte Recipe

Home

Description

A yummy homemade pumpkin spice latte recipe that will forsure make you not want to go to coffee shop again.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons canned pumpkin purée
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar, or to taste
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ¼ teaspoon pumpkin pie spice
  • 1 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger brewed espresso

Steps:

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Whisk milk, pumpkin purée, sugar, vanilla, and pumpkin pie spice together in a small saucepan; warm over medium heat, whisking constantly, until hot and frothy.
  3. Pour brewed espresso into a mug; top with pumpkin spiced milk.
  4. Enjoy! :p

Nutrition Facts (per serving)

  • 244 calories
  • 5g fat
  • 40g carbs
  • 9g Protein
