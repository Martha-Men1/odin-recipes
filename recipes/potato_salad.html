Potato Salad Recipe

Home

Description

Yummy simple potato salad recipe, that will knock your socks off!

Ingredients:

  • 3 red potatoes
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 ½ cups creamy salad dressing

Steps:

  1. Gather the ingredients.
  2. Bring a small pot of salted water to a boil. Add potatoes; cook until tender but still firm, about 15 minutes. Drain and cool.
  3. Place eggs in a saucepan, and cover completely with cold water. Bring water to a boil. Cover, remove from heat, and let eggs stand in hot water for 10 to 12 minutes. Remove from hot water, and cool.
  4. Peel the eggs and cut around the egg white; keep the yolk whole.
  5. Dice egg whites and potatoes. Combine in a mixing bowl and add salad dressing
  6. Toss potatoes and egg; crumble egg yolk on top and serve chilled.

Nutrition Facts (per serving)

  • 557 calories
  • 36g fat
  • 50g carbs
  • 8g Protein
